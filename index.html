We are dedicated to helping you find delicious coffee and good vibes in our city.

Arctos Coffee Roasters

Arctos is near Gonzaga and is a favorite location for students

The warm vibes encourage productive studying or a meangingful chat with friends. The coffee is no joke either.

Located in the Logan Neighborhood, Arctos is accesible to most of Spokane.

Indaba Coffee Roasters

Indaba Coffee Roasters is one of the premier coffee roasters in Spokane!

Known for their community engagement and friendly staff, Indaba is beloved by many coffee lovers in the city.

With 3 locations (and a fourth on the way), Indaba has a cozy table for you!
